Our Story

More Than a Boutique.
A Village Institution.

Bella & Co opened on Queen Street in 2013 with a simple belief: every woman deserves to feel beautiful, regardless of her budget. Nestled in the heart of Northumberland's historic village center, we've spent over a decade building something rare — a boutique where you walk in as a stranger and leave as a regular.

We travel the country sourcing figure-flattering styles, on-trend accessories, and thoughtfully curated jewelry that you won't find in chain stores. Our selections celebrate real bodies, real budgets, and real personal style.

"We wanted a place where women could look expensive without spending a fortune — and feel genuinely seen while they shopped."

From our brick-and-mortar roots to a growing online presence, we remain a brick-and-mortar mainstay of the Pennsylvania heartland — and we're just getting started.

The Experience

The Philosophy

Look expensive without the price tag.

Trend-forward but timeless. Affordable but never cheap-looking. We curate every rack and every jewelry display with one question in mind: Would we wear this ourselves?

If the answer is yes, it earns its place on the floor.
