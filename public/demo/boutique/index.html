Our Story

Style that knows who you are.

Thread & Stone grew out of a simple belief: every woman deserves a place where style is personal, not generic. We opened our doors in 2013 in the village center of Millerton, PA, with one goal — to make great style accessible without the distance or the pressure of a mall.

What started as a small boutique has grown into a full shopping experience. We carry thoughtfully chosen apparel, accessories, and a dedicated Jewelry Bar with fine and fashion jewelry — all under one roof. Our team knows fashion, but more importantly, we know our customers.

We specialize in figure-flattering fits and personal styling. When you walk in, you're not browsing alone. You have someone in your corner.

More Than A Boutique

The Jewelry Bar

Thread & Stone is two experiences in one. Our dedicated Jewelry Bar sits within the boutique — a curated space for fine and fashion jewelry, from delicate everyday pieces to statement accessories for your biggest moments.

Whether you're shopping for yourself or finding the perfect gift, our jewelry specialists will help you find what speaks to you. We stock rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets from independent and boutique designers.

  • Fine & fashion jewelry from boutique designers
  • Dedicated jewelry specialist on staff
  • Gift wrapping and personal shopping available
  • Custom styling appointments for special occasions
