Born & Raised

A Valley
Institution

"We grew up in this valley. We opened this shop for the people who grew up here too."

On the Roll started in 2008 as a small counter operation on Queen Street. No franchise, no formula — just a family recipe book, a commitment to real ingredients, and a belief that a great sandwich can make someone's day better.

We use Dietz & Watson meats because quality matters. We bake fresh every morning because shortcuts show. We're open Tuesday through Saturday because that's what works for a real neighborhood deli, not a corporate location count.

Northumberland isn't just where we work. It's home.

What Sets Us Apart

The Philly Roll
Difference

Not a hoagie roll. Not a kaiser. A real Philly roll — chewy crust, soft center, holds up to anything. Here's why it matters.

🥖
Authentic Philly Rolls

Delivered fresh every morning from a Pennsylvania bakery. The crust has the right snap. The interior has the right give. Every sub starts here.

🥩
Dietz & Watson Meats

We've carried Dietz & Watson since day one. No mystery meats. No cheap fillers. Real deli-quality cuts sliced fresh at the counter — every single day.

🧀
Real Cheese, Always

Sharp provolone, fresh mozzarella, smoked Gruyere. We don't use the plastic stuff. Cheese is a texture and a flavor — it should taste like something.

🌿
Fresh. Every Morning.

Produce prepped same-day. Sides made in-house. Rolls baked fresh. We don't hold over. If it's from yesterday, it doesn't go on a sandwich. Simple rule.

"You can taste the difference when someone cares about the roll."
