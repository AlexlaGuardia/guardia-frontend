Since the Beginning

A Family Thing

Stack House Deli started the way every good deli does — with someone's mom making a better sandwich than anything else in town. We opened our doors because the neighborhood deserved a real deli: not a chain, not a gas station counter, but an actual spot where they know your order.

We slice everything fresh. We make the sides ourselves. The bread comes from a Philly roll bakery because that's the only roll worth putting a sandwich on, and we refuse to compromise on that.

"Our regulars kept us going through everything. We built this place for them, and we still run it the same way — like family."

Tuesday through Saturday, 10am to 7pm. Stop in, eat well, bring the kids.

Difference
